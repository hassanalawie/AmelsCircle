My Philosophy

Why I'm Doing What I'm Doing

Not so much of a shocker; but living with depression is not easy. It is very lonely and sometimes gets you into trouble. Growing up I was taught that having a mental illness was not something to display, mostly by my peers but also by my family. I felt like I was really different and odd. I always wished that someone would come into my life and reassure me about how I was feeling. Looking back at my experiences the only part that I wish had been different was having someone to talk about my feelings with. ​

I created this website because I want to help others who feel the same way I did, to me having someone to talk to would have made dealing with my mental health issues less lonely. I would like to start the conversation about using support groups to assist with mental illness. It is a universal thought that the only way to support someone dealing with a mental illness is by seeking professional help. This may be true in some aspects; however, the use of support groups and discussion circles can help those who are not ready to seek out professional help by allowing them to understand their feelings and to know that they are not alone in their battles.

So welcome to my circle, feel free to stay a while 😊
